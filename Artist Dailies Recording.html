Instructions on how to save video recordings (Zoom/G-Meet) of the Artist dailies to the server

ⓘ Avoid storing any recordings on the Shogrid portal or any other online services like Google Drives or wetransfer.

The Production team should share the recording with the Data IO team after the dailies are finished.

Based on the Show or the information given by the Production team or the Supervisor, the Data IO team will download and copy to the appropriate folder on the Storage:

/media/vis/sg/prod/"*SHOWCODE*"/reference/recordings/YYYYMMDD

Production team has access to the Aspera page via the link provided below and can manage the recorded data to be uploaded from their laptops or conference room systems:

IBM Aspera All files >> Artist_Dailies_Recording >> YYYYMMDD

Please inform the dataio@viskefi.com staff when the recording has been uploaded so they can download it and put it in the appropriate location on the server, which should be under:

/media/vis/sg/prod/"*SHOWCODE*"/reference/recordings/YYYYMMDD
