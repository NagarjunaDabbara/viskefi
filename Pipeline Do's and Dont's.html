Plug-ins

Do not use plug-ins not approved by production

There might be scenarios where you might be testing some third-party plug-in due to personal interest. However, having those enabled while working on production files might lead to dependency about those plug-ins included in your scene files, which can cause issues when the files reach the studio infrastructure.

As a general rule of thumb:

  • ✔ DO NOT enable auto-load of plug-ins that are not approved by production
