Workflow - FX Dept

    INPUTS:

  • FX have ANIM and MATCHMOVE as Upstream Dept’s.
  • FX imports .abc from ANIM and camera,BG plate from MATCHMOVE as formats.
  • FX have .abc, .fbx, .xgen as an input formats from up-stream departments.
  • FX uses Houdini, Maya as working environment and for internal checking FX use NUKE.

    OUTPUTS:

  • FX have .ass, .vdb, .abc as output foormats for down-stream departments.
  • FX exports .abc(mesh) and seq alembic for Lighting .
  • FX also exports .vdb for volumetric if Lighting requires.

    ISSUES

  • Importing data from up-stream Dept’s should be in .abc for char and .obj, .abc for BG needed.
  • Camera must be in .fbx or .abc formats.
